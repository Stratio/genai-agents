# Warm Magazine

Editorial warmth: a sturdy terracotta as the anchor, a condensed display
face against a classic serif body. The result reads like a monthly
long-form piece rather than a corporate output. Built for storytelling,
communications and marketing content.

## Optional extensions

- **Motion budget**: `expressive` (the magazine tone invites considered
  motion: deliberate reveals, not mechanical fades)
- **Border radius**: `0px` (editorial geometry is hard-edged)
- **Dark mode variant**: not shipped
- **Chart sequential**: base color `#8a3324`

## Tone family

`warm-magazine`.

## Best used for

- Internal communications newsletters and long-form briefings
- Marketing and brand storytelling
- Reports where narrative matters as much as data

## Anti-patterns

- Do not soften the display face with letter-spacing or lighter weights —
  the theme relies on a muscular condensed headline.
- Do not pair with cool blue backgrounds; the warm anchor must dominate.
- Do not use for legal or compliance documents — the voice is too
  narrative for regulated tone.
